About US

FOUNDATION FOR GIRL’S HOPE (FGH)_is a child centred, Non-government Organization registered 2019 under the Non-governmental Organizations Act 2002. Given registration No. ooNGO/R/096 to work in Tanzania mainland. The organization focuses on the protection of children and elimination of all forms of abuse and stigmatization to children, advocating issues of children in the community and awareness raising on basic children’s rights, gender equality and equity. Before formal registration in July 2019, Foundation for Girls Hope started as a community group where as archievements were made on emphasing education rights and reporting all forms of abuse and discrimination which geared the advancement to an NGO so as to expand the vision inorder to reach many children and young girls.

Vision Statement

Equal rights and opportunities are provided to all girl’s and young women, inorder to develop to their full potentials in life.

Mission Statement

We promoting gender equality and equity, providing education, create safe and supportive environment to girls and we link them to available social assistance system and structures.
