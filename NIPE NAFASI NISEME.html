PROJECT PROPOSAL

[2.] ‘’Nipe Nafasi Niseme’’ (Ujasiri wangu Silaha yangu)



Nipe nafasi niseme-is a national campaign which has a mission of rescuing more children and breaking the silent of victims of abuse, The campaign is constructed to capture and addresses agendas of children, which have been neglected with some parents and community members as well. This is a 3 years’ campaign which aim to create more awareness to children and the whole communities to report all forms of abuses in domestics, at schools, either child-child or adult-child etc. recently domestic abuse is highly occurring in the society and is often not reported, all cases are informal closed, as family member’s agreeing themselves. Sometimes the abuse continues and the children’s remains with the pain. Many studies shows that, children who have experienced abuse and violance of different forms, they are not confidence, abuse and violance erodes the strong natural foundation that help children in leading a healthy safepath from childhood. This can be notified as many children express fear in introducing themselves, appearing infront of new people, high privacy, trusting few people or no anyone in their inner trust cycle, also they are not confident in presentation and last but not least fear to break the silent while there is risks of abuse. Getting to know, courage is a weapon for children for sustainable growth. Courage determines how children and young people can handle challenges, how they can best relate to others and make informed choices, to develop in a healthy way. Helps also to builds strong relationships and adapt to changes in dealing with life challenges. Courage can be eroded to children’s life through many ways including a continuation of abusive actions to children, lack parental skills which demonstrating positive parenting etc..
